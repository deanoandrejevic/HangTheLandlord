HANG THE LANDLORD

RULES!

This game is based of the quiz show "Who Wants To Be a Millionaire?" But this will purely test your music knowledge, ranging from heavy metal to 60's jazz so be prepared!

  • The rules are simple!
  • You have 20 questions to answer
  • They range in difficulty and genre
  • If you get one wrong Game Over!
  • If you get all 20 right you win!
  • But the most important rule of all!

Most importantly have fun!
