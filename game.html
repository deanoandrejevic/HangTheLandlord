HANG THE LANDLORD

RULES!

This game is based of the quiz show "Who Wants To Be a Millionaire?" But this will purely test your music knowledge, ranging from heavy metal to 60's jazz so be prepared!

  • You will have 30 seconds to answer each questions, if you do not answer in time you lose!
  • Every 5 questions you will reach a checkpoint which if you fail will be your minimum score.
  • After each checkpoint the difficulty will increase.
  • You will have one hint per game, so choice wisely when too use it.
  • The more answers you answer correctly the further you will move up the ladder.
  • You can give up at anytime will lock in your score at your current position
  • Answer all 20 questions correctly and you win!

Most importantly have fun!
